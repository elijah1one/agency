About

A little story about our humble origins.

  • 2009-2011

    A Small But Dedicated Start

    We began with Ed as the one man team with a vision. He wanted to bring his love of digital design to the world. As a small business entrepreneur himself, he decided to help Mom and Pop shops get a professional web image.

  • March 2011

    An Agency Arises

    Ed had some success with his first few projects. It became too much for one man to handle, so he linked up with a team. Three more members were brought aboard to serve a valuable set of clientele.

  • December 2014

    Arriving to a New Digital Destination

    A few years down the line the team had landed several consistent clients and worked on modernizing its approach to design and marketing. The agency became a well-oiled machine.

  • July 2015

    Phase Two Expansion

    The agency decided go beyond local markets and serve clients in different cities, states, and even countries. We are continuing to expand our global presence.
